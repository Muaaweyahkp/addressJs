Early Morning

Wake Up: 5:00 AM - Wake up and perform morning face and hair care routine.

Fajr Prayer at Mosque: 5:30 AM - Perform Fajr prayer at the mosque.

Workout and Running: 5:45 AM - 7:00 AM - Engage in workout and running.

Alternative: If unable to run outside, consider a home workout or yoga session.

Morning

Shower: 7:00 AM - Take a shower after the workout.

Post-Shower Routine: 7:30 AM - Complete any additional face and hair care needed.

Homework: 8:00 AM - Spend 1 hour on homework or other tasks.

Alternative: If no homework, use this time for reading or personal projects.

Breakfast: 9:15 AM - Have a nutritious breakfast.

Alternative: If in a rush, opt for a quick healthy snack or smoothie.

Travel to Work: 9:30 AM - Commute to the workplace.

Alternative: If working from home, use this time for a brief walk or additional work preparation.

Start Work: 10:00 AM - Begin the workday.

Midday

Dhuhr Prayer at Mosque: 1:00 PM - Perform Dhuhr prayer at the mosque.

Continue Work: 1:30 PM - Continue working.

Afternoon

Asr Prayer at Mosque: 4:30 PM - Perform Asr prayer at the mosque.

Finish Work: 5:00 PM - Complete work tasks and travel back home.

Evening

Maghrib Prayer at Mosque: 6:30 PM - Perform Maghrib prayer at the mosque.

Surah Al-Thabaraka Recitation: 7:00 PM - Recite Surah Al-Thabaraka after Maghrib prayer.

Family Time: 7:30 PM - Spend quality time with family.

Isha Prayer at Mosque: 8:30 PM - Perform Isha prayer at the mosque.

Dinner: 9:00 PM - Have dinner.

Entertainment: 9:30 PM - Watch movies, series, or spend time on social media.

Night

Reading: 10:30 PM - Read a book for 30 minutes.

Night Routine: 11:00 PM - Perform night face and hair care routine.

Sleep: 11:15 PM - Go to bed and aim for a restful sleep.
